### Full description

VELLIN helps you interrupt distraction loops, protect your attention, and build a calmer daily rhythm around your phone.

Use VELLIN to:

- start focus sessions with stronger interruption friction
- choose the apps that distract you most
- view your own focus progress, streaks, and reclaimed time
- generate a personal reduction roadmap based on your tracked activity
- unlock deeper Forecast insight cards and prevention suggestions with Pro
- connect with friends and compare progress on a leaderboard

VELLIN is designed for people who want less reactive scrolling and more control over how they spend their time and attention.

Core features:

- Focus sessions and in-app break reminders
- Distraction app selection and block guidance
- Reality Check and screen-time style insight features
- Craving Forecast and prevention suggestions
- Personal reduction roadmap
- Progress tracking, milestones, and achievements
- Account sync and optional social features

Permissions used on Android:

- Notification permission:
  - used for optional daily nudges, focus reminders, and break reminders
- Usage Access:
  - used only if the user grants it, so VELLIN can show the user their own app-usage totals inside focus insights and Reality Check style views

VELLIN includes a free core experience. Premium subscription features are being prepared and should only be advertised in-store once Google Play Billing is fully live in the released build.
