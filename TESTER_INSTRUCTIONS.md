# Tester Instructions

- an Android phone
- a Google account
- the Play test link from me

## Steps
1. Open the Play closed-test opt-in link.
2. Accept the invitation.
3. Install `VELLIN`.
4. Open the app once to make sure it installs properly.
5. Stay opted in for the next 14 days.
