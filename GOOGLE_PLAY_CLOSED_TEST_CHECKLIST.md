# Google Play Closed Test Checklist

## What This Is
Because this is a new personal Google Play developer account, Google requires a closed test before public release.

## What You Need
- 1 app created in Play Console
- 1 Android App Bundle (`.aab`)
- 12 real testers
- 14 continuous days with those testers opted in

## Simple Order
1. Create the `VELLIN` app in Play Console.
2. Create a closed testing track.
3. Upload the Android release build when it is ready.
4. Add at least 12 testers.
5. Send them the opt-in link.
6. Make sure they actually install and stay opted in.
7. Wait 14 continuous days.
8. Apply for production access.

## Important
- Do not rely on flaky testers.
- If you drop below the required tester threshold, you risk wasting time.
- Pick testers who are likely to stay opted in for the full 14 days.

## Best Tester Sources
- family
- relatives
- classmates
- trusted online volunteers
- Android testing communities

## Safer Strategy
- Try to recruit more than 12, for example 15 to 20.
- That gives you a buffer in case some people stop responding.
